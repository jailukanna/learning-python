Stores and Products

In this assignment, we'll continue our use of `many-to-many` relationships. We will build two models: (1) `Stores` and (2) `Products`. We will then setup a many-to-many relationship, so that many stores may contain many products, and many products may exist in many stores. If confused, please see `anthologies` assignment, or the Django Many-to-Many section.
